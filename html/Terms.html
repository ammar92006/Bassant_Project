How to Apply and Implementation

When you select an order from our list of available delivery locations, you can place your order by clicking on the “Place Order” button. Please note that it is important that you review the information you enter and correct any errors before clicking on the “Place Order” button, as once clicked, input errors cannot be corrected.

Upon receipt of your order, Bloomy will begin to process the order, and we will send a message on the final page stating that your order has been received and successfully sent to the flower shop. When you make an unauthorized payment, you will be returned to the previous page on the website, and we will not be obligated to provide the services.
