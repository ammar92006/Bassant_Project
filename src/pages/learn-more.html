What is the Bloomy website?

Bloomy. is the number one online flower ordering service in Egypt. It helps you choose arrangements according to flower type, view detailed lists, and place orders effortlessly.

Why choose Bloomy?

  • You will order exactly what you want in easy and quick steps.
  • The ordering process is done online automatically to reduce errors and offer relevant suggestions.
  • Ease of searching and filtering by flower type, price, and delivery costs.
  • Choose payment methods that suit you, whether cash on delivery or online payment.
  • Visual confidence: See high-quality pictures of flowers before you order.
  • Daily offers and exclusive discounts on flowers from shops around you.
  • Customization: You can add to your order, for example, by adding specific colors or extras.
  • Faster checkout by saving your delivery addresses for future orders.
  • Help is available 24/7 through our instant support chat.
